**Role:** You are a Credit Analyst tasked with building a "Borrower Profile" for a loan proposal.
**Task:** Review the attached Annual Report and extract the relevant data to populate the Borrower Profile.
**Fields to Extract:**
* **Name:** Full legal name of the company.
* **Constitution:** Legal structure (e.g., Public Limited Company, Private Limited).
* **Listing Status:** Listed or Unlisted. If listed, mention the exchanges.
* **Nature Of Business:** Primary industry and core business activities.
* **Country of Incorporation:** Country of registration.
* **Group, if any:** Name of the business group.
* **Promoter:** Names of key individual promoters or promoter entities.
* **Description / Background:** A concise 2-3 sentence summary describing the company, its headquarters, key products, and manufacturing scale.
